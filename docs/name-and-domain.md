### Name and Domain Suggestions

#### Context

- **Idea:** Web app for bachata dancers, teachers, and DJs to link Spotify, add songs by link, manually annotate song structure (counts, breaks, accents, sections), and browse a community catalog of analyzed songs.
- **Target audience:** Bachata dancers (social + performance), teachers, DJs — global, English-first.
- **Key associations:** Bachata, counts, rhythm, timing, breakdown, catalog, community, dance, music analysis.

#### Name Options

| # | Name | Style | Domain Suggestions | Social Handle | Notes |
|---|------|-------|--------------------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | BachataBeat | Compound | bachatabeat.com, bachatabeat.io, getbachatabeat.com | @bachatabeat | Evokes the beat/count — core to bachata. Short, memorable. |
| 2 | CountMeIn | Evocative | countmein.com, countmein.app, getcountmein.com | @countmein | Play on "count" (music) and inclusion. Appeals to dancers joining the community. |

| 5 | BachataBreak | Compound | bachatabreak.com, bachatabreak.io | @bachatabreak | Direct: bachata + break (the breakdown). Clear but "break" might imply "break from work." |
| 6 | SyncFlow | Made-up | syncflow.com, syncflow.io, getsyncflow.com | @syncflow | Evokes syncing to the music, flowing with the beat. Modern, tech-friendly. |

#### Top Recommendation

**BachataBeat** — Clear, memorable, and specific to bachata. The word "beat" ties directly to counts and timing, which is the core of the product. Short enough for social handles and domains. If the .com is taken, .io is a good alternative for a tech product.
